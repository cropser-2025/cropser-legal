# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 29th November 2025

## 1. Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- Phone numbers for WhatsApp communication
- Business information (name, location, products)
- Transaction details (products, prices, quantities)

### Agricultural Data  
- Product listings (crops, fertilizers, seeds)
- Location data for matching buyers/sellers
- Transaction history

### Automated Information
- Message timestamps and interaction data
- Device information for service optimization

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### Service Provision
- Matching agro-dealers with buyers/sellers
- Facilitating communication via WhatsApp
- Processing transactions and payments
- Providing customer support

### Platform Improvement
- Analyzing usage patterns to improve service
- Developing new features for agricultural trading
- Ensuring platform security and integrity

## 3. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We only share data:

### Essential Sharing
- With other agro-dealers for matching purposes (phone numbers for connection)
- With payment processors for transaction completion
- As required by Kenyan law

### Anonymous Data
- Aggregated, anonymized data for market analysis
- Agricultural trend reporting without personal identifiers

## 4. Data Security

We implement:
- Secure API connections for all data transfers
- Regular security assessments
- Access controls to protect your information

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate information
- Delete your account and data
- Opt-out of communications
